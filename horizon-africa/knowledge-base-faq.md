## 24. What is symmetrical Fibre?

Symmetrical Fibre means your upload speed is the same as your download speed.

Example:

- 100 Mbps Download
- 100 Mbps Upload

This is ideal for businesses, remote workers and content creators.

## 25. What is asymmetrical Fibre?

Asymmetrical Fibre has a faster download speed than upload speed.

Example:

- 20 Mbps Download
- 10 Mbps Upload

This is suitable for everyday home internet use.
